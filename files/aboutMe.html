ABOUT ME
Programmer by Day.
Photographer by Night.
Welcome to my world. My name is Priten Vora, and my goal in life is to make really cool stuff that makes people happy. I am a student, programmer, photographer, and aspiring video game designer. Game programming combines technical knowledge and proficiency with creativity, teamwork, and communication, and I firmly believe that if crafted well, gaming can be the most effective platform in humankind's history for engaging, inspiring, and entertaining people. By being a computer science student, I'm working toward my goal of being a game programmer, but I also enjoy programming in general, so I often take on programming projects outside of the scope of video game creation. You will find many such projects in my programming section. Being that video games are an amalgamation of technology and art, I've always had quite an appreciation for creative, artistic things, and in order to remind myself not to lose that while being a programmer, I being getting into photography during the latter years of my high school career. Since then, photography has opened doors for me and allowed me to make friends with people I'd probably have never met or talked to otherwise. Currently, I mainly photograph concerts for a variety of online publications, as well as occasionally doing portraits and headshots.
